About Me

Hello! I am Raaeid Akram. I’m a professional web designer with a passion for turning ideas into immersive online experiences. My mission is to design websites that not only look stunning but also function seamlessly, providing users with a memorable and intuitive journey.

With a deep understanding of design principles, I specialize in creating responsive, modern, and visually engaging websites tailored to the unique needs of businesses and individuals. From wireframes and prototypes to fully functional designs, I focus on every detail to ensure that the final product reflects your vision and resonates with your audience.

I’m constantly learning and evolving, staying ahead of the latest web design trends and technologies to bring fresh ideas to my projects. Whether it's developing a sleek portfolio, building a user-friendly business site, or designing a robust e-commerce platform, I combine creativity with technical expertise to deliver exceptional results.

Collaboration is at the heart of my process. I love working closely with clients to understand their goals, ensuring each project is as unique as the story behind it.

When I’m not designing, I enjoy exploring the art of photography, experimenting with new design tools, and seeking inspiration from the world around me.

Let’s create something extraordinary together!


Over the years, I’ve developed a diverse skill set that bridges creativity and functionality, allowing me to bring unique visions to life. Here’s an in-depth look at the tools and languages I work with:

HTML – 30%
HTML forms the backbone of every website I create. I specialize in writing clean, semantic, and SEO-friendly code to ensure that every page is well-structured and optimized for performance. Whether building static pages or interactive layouts, I rely on HTML as the foundation for all web development projects.

CSS – 30%
CSS allows me to transform basic structures into visually appealing, responsive designs. I have expertise in creating layouts that adapt seamlessly across devices, applying animations, transitions, and custom styles to elevate the user experience. My CSS skills ensure that every website stands out while remaining intuitive and functional.

Java – 20%
I use Java for backend development and creating dynamic, interactive features on websites. My experience includes building logic-driven applications, handling server-side functionality, and integrating APIs to provide richer user interactions.

Visual Basic (VB) – 10%
While not my primary focus, VB has been invaluable in creating user-friendly tools and applications tailored to specific needs. I leverage VB for projects that require quick prototyping or simple functional applications.

C++ – 10%
C++ gives me a deeper understanding of programming principles and logic development. I apply this knowledge to problem-solving, optimizing performance, and enhancing the functionality of web-related projects when needed.
